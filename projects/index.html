Feature Roadmap

Vib Timer is a WatchOS app that sends subtle vibration reminders to your wrist. Whether you're concentrating on work, practicing meditation, engaging in a workout, or handling everyday tasks, Vib Timer helps you stay aware of the passing time without distractions.

Every user enjoys full access to all available features — no subscriptions & no ads.

Below is an overview of the features planned for future releases:

Recently Added

✓Start a 5-minute timer with vibrations every 30 seconds – New!
✓One-Tap Control: Start, pause, or reset the timer with a single tap. – New!
✓Real-Time Display: See the elapsed time counting up. –New!
